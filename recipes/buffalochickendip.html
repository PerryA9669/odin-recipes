Buffalo Chicken Dip

A tasty dip to feed the whole room!

Ingredients

  • 2(10 ounce)cans chunk chicken, drained
  • 3/4 cup hot pepper sauce (franks red hot)
  • 2(8 ounce) packages of cream cheese
  • 1 cup ranch dressing
  • 1.5 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 bunch celery, cut into 4-inch pieces
  • 1 (8 ounce) box chicken-flavored crackers

Directions

  1. Heat chicken and hot pepper sauce in a skillet over medium heat until heated through, 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in cream cheese and ranch dressing. Cook and stir until well blended and warm.

  2. Mix in 1/2 of the cheddar cheese, and transfer the mixture to a slow cooker. Sprinkle the remaining cheese on top.

  3. Cover and cook on low until dip is hot and bubbly, about 35 minutes

  4. Serve with celery sticks and crackers
