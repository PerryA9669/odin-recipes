Chili

A yummy meal based with beans and beef. Perfect with fritos ontop, espically when it's cold outside!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Beef
  • Onion, chopped
  • Canned tomato sauce(15 ounce)
  • Can of stewed tomatoes(15 ounce)
  • Canned kidney beans(15 ounce)
  • 1 pinch of Chili powder
  • 1 pinch of Garlic powder
  • salt and pepper
  • 1.5 cups of water

Directions

  1. Place ground beef and onion in a large saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir until meat is browned and onion is tender, about 5-7 minutes

  2. Stir in tomato sauce, kideny beans, stewed tomatos with juice, and water. Season with chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover and let simmer for 15 minutes

  3. Enjoy! Add fritos and cheese to taste
